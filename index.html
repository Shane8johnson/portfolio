Lights On Jupiter

Lights On Jupiter is a 501 (c)(3) nonprofit organization dedicated to Lighten, Brighten, and Heighten the spirits in our community! Each year we bring joy to the community with a musical display of Lights in Marche (North Little Rock), Arkansas consisting of 2 neighboring houses with over 50,000 flashing lights to your favorite Christmas songs, 80s mixes, Christmas remixes and more! There will certainly be something for the whole family to enjoy! Use the Light Show Control Panel to choose from any of our 15 readily available songs.
